# Brand Identity Kit - WebLynx Création

## 1. Brand Essence
**Name**: WebLynx Création  
**Tagline**: "Le lien numérique vers votre succès" (The digital link to your success)  
**Tone**: Confident, Sleek, Innovative, Professional.  
**Mission**: To elevate Quebec businesses through premium, high-performance digital solutions.

## 2. Color Palette
A high-contrast "Dark Mode" aesthetic designed to convey modernity and technology.

| Color Name | Hex Code | Usage |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Obsidian Dark** | `#0a0a0c` | Main background, deep void feel. |
| **Surface Gray** | `#121216` | Cards, panels, secondary backgrounds. |
| **Neon Cyan** | `#00f2ea` | Primary Call-to-Actions, highlights, glows. |
| **Electric Purple** | `#7000ff` | Secondary accents, gradients, "creative" feel. |
| **Mist White** | `#f3f4f6` | Primary Text (high legibility). |
| **Slate Gray** | `#9ca3af` | Secondary Text. |

## 3. Typography
**Headings**: *Poppins* (Weights: 600, 700) - Geometric, friendly but authoritative.  
**Body**: *Inter* (Weights: 300, 400, 500) - Clean, highly readable interface font.

## 4. Logo Concept
**Symbol**: A stylized abstract Lynx head formed by geometric circuitry lines or interconnected nodes (representing the "Web" and "Link").
- **Style**: Minimalist monoline.
- **Icon Color**: Gradient from Neon Cyan to Electric Purple.
- **Wordmark**: "WebLynx" in Bold Sans-Serif (White), "Création" in lighter weight (Gray) below or beside.

## 5. AI Image Generation Prompt
Use this prompt to generate matching visuals for the website (e.g., in Midjourney or DALL-E 3):

> **Prompt**:
> "A futuristic digital agency workspace background, abstract representation of web development and connectivity, dark mode aesthetic, isometric 3D glowing nodes connecting with neon cyan and electric purple lines, deep obsidian black background, glassmorphism textures, sleek, minimalist, high-end tech vibe, 8k resolution, cinematic lighting, shallow depth of field, no text."

## 6. Social Media & Favicon
- **Favicon**: The "W" from the logo inside a rounded square with a dark background and cyan border.
- **Social Headers**: A mesh gradient background (Cyan/Purple/Dark) with the tagline centered in white.
